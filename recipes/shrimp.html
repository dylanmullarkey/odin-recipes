Shrimp Recipe

Description

Shrimp cocktail can be an expensive meal, but it's a great main course or appetizer for any party. They are a great source of protein and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Frozen shrimp
  • Ketchup
  • Horseradish

Steps

  1. Take your frozen shrimp and boil them in water until orange
  2. While they boil, mix ketchup with horseradish

That's it! This is my go to recipe for making shrimp cocktail. I hope you enjoy as much as I do!
