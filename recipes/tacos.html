Smoothie Recipe

Description

Tacos are a great meal for a family, as you can customize them however you want. This may not be the most authetic way, but it's easy to make and can feed several people.

Ingredients

  • 1lb. lean ground beef
  • Taco shells/tortillas
  • Optional:
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • Taco Sauce
  • Hot Sauce
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Unwrap your beef and put it into a pan on medium-high heat. Cook as long as needed until all meat is brown.
  2. Once done, there will be a lot of grease in the pan. If you have a big spoon, you can seperate this and put the grease in the trash. Do not pour it down your sink.
  3. Prepare any other ingredients you want to use.
  4. You are done! If you are using tortillas, it's a good idea to throw them in the microwave for 10 seconds to have some warm burritos

I hope you enjoy as much as I do!
